Arrival by Air

Salzburg W. A. Mozart Airport is only four kilometers from the city center, a fifteen-minute taxi or public transportation ride. Direct travel from the airport to Salzburg Congress is available on bus line 2.

Arrival by train

Salzburg Congress is 5 minutes away by foot from Salzburg train station. The centre of the city is 15 minutes away by foot from here. In addition, you can also use regional train or S-Bahn from some of the nearby cities to reach Salzburg. You can reach the conference centre by lines 1, 2, 3, 5, 6, 25, and 840 from the train station.

Arrival by car

Salzburg is the hub of the European motorway network: the A1 Vienna-Salzburg, the A8 Munich-Salzburg, and the A10 Villach-Salzburg meet in Salzburg. Don't forget to purchase a vignette for Austrian motorways when traveling to Salzburg. Parking is situated in the vicinity of the Salzburg Congress.
